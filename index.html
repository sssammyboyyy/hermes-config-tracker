Website Gaps

⚠ TTFB 2.1s — Server Response Critically Slow

2.6x slower than 0.8s threshold. Backend infrastructure needs optimization. Impact: losing ~30% of mobile visitors before content loads.

⚠ LCP 2,627ms — 53% Mobile Bounce Risk

53% of mobile visitors abandon sites over 3 seconds. At 2.6s, dangerously close to the cliff. Fix: optimize hero images, defer non-critical CSS/JS.

⚠ No Meta Description

Google generates its own snippet. Losing up to 30% click-through from search. Each property page needs a unique, keyword-rich meta description.

⚠ No OpenGraph Tags

Social shares appear as plain links. Every share is free marketing — wasted. Adding OG tags takes 30 minutes and can double social click-through.

⚠ No Live Chat

73% of visitors expect instant engagement. No chat = lost bookings from late-night browsers and international guests (especially Germany).
