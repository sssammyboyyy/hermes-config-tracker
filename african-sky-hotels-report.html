Website Gaps

⚠ TTFB 2.1s — Server Response Critically Slow

2.6x slower than the 0.8s threshold. This indicates backend infrastructure issues. Google penalizes slow TTFB in rankings. Impact: losing ~30% of mobile visitors before content even starts loading.

⚠ LCP 2,627ms — 53% Mobile Bounce Risk

Google research: 53% of mobile visitors abandon sites that take longer than 3 seconds. At 2.6s, African Sky is dangerously close to this cliff.

⚠ 23 External Resources

Each external CSS/JS file blocks rendering. This alone could shave 1-2 seconds off load time with proper optimization.

⚠ No Meta Description

Google generates its own snippet for search results without a meta description. For a hotel, this means losing the chance to show rates, location, or unique value — reducing click-through by up to 30%.

⚠ No OpenGraph Tags

Shares on Facebook/LinkedIn appear as plain links. Every social share is free marketing — but without OG tags, those shares look unprofessional and get ignored.

⚠ H1 Not Keyword-Optimized

"WHERE WOULD YOU LIKE TO STAY?" has no keyword focus. Should include target keywords like "Book Hotels in South Africa."

⚠ No Live Chat

73% of visitors expect instant engagement. A guest with a question at 11pm can't get an answer until morning — and by then, they've booked elsewhere.
